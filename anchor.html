ANCHOR

Your safe space for vital information and legacy wishes.

Our Goal

Anchor is your safe space that holds all of your important data and organize your online life, emergency and health info, and final wishes with privacy-first sharing controls for trusted contacts when it matters most.

Problem

For people like us living alone or away from family, there's often no reliable, accessible way to store and share vital information — from emergency contacts and medical details to personal notes, digital assets, and legacy wishes.

In a crisis, this missing link can create confusion, delays, or loss of critical data.

Features

What Anchor aims to achieve

  • ✓

  • ✓

    Store & share medical info and emergency contacts

  • ✓

  • ✓

  • ✓

    Vault personal memories & messages for trusted people
